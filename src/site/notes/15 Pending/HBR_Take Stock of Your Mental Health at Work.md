It’s important to reflect on the relationship between your work and your mental health. The first section of this tool focuses on the overall state of your mental health right now. Then, you’ll consider how your work affects your mental health, and vice versa. In the last section, you’ll ask yourself what a better version of this relationship could look like.

## Reflecting on Your Mental Health

How is your mental health these days? Try using the following prompts:

·      “These days I feel ...”

·      “My body feels ...”

·      “I’m struggling to ...”

·      “I feel best when ...”

·      “I feel worst when ...”

·      “When I think about the week, I feel ...”

·      “When I’m at work, I struggle to ...”

·      “When I’m not at work, I struggle to ...”

·      “Recently, my sleep/nutrition/physical activity/self-care has been ...”
